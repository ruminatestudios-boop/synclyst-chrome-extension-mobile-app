    From photo to live in 4 steps

    Do this to get your products on your stores—no typing required.

    Snap or upload

    Point your camera at the product or choose a photo from your device. One clear image is enough—we build your draft from that single shot.

    Review draft

    We draft title, description, category, and tags from your photo. Review the listing, adjust any field you like, and continue only when it feels right.

    Push to store

    Send the draft to Shopify, Etsy, eBay, TikTok Shop, or Amazon—saved as a draft in your store. Nothing appears on your storefront until you publish from your admin.

    Publish ready

    Open your store admin when you’re ready. Polish the draft, tweak details if needed, then hit Publish. Your product goes live to buyers as soon as you confirm.
