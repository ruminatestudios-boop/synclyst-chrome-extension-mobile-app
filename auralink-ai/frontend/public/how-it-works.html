How SyncLyst works

Scan on your phone, review on desktop, then Magic Fill your marketplace listing.

Flow

Session & QR Pairing

The extension keeps one session id. Your phone opens the same session via QR or link so photos and AI results land in the extension.

Optional: hide or blur sensitive text in your photos before you scan (prices, addresses) if you share screens.

Magic Fill listing forms

Steps

1. Pair your phone

Open the extension QR, scan with your phone, upload or take photos. The session id stays in sync with the /snap page.

2. Choose a marketplace

Shopify, eBay, Etsy, Shopee, Depop, or Vinted — the review form matches what that platform needs.

3. Review & fill

Edit title, description, and price, then use Magic Fill on an open listing tab. Save on the marketplace as usual.

Tip: keep the pairing tab and extension on the same session id so the QR and URL always match.
