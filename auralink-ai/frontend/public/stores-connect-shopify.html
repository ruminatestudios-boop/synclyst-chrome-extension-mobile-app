If Shopify mentioned redirect URL, callback, or the app

That usually means the app’s callback address in Shopify (Partners / Dev Dashboard) doesn’t match what this site uses after you click Install—not that your store name is spelled wrong. The store owner or whoever manages the app needs to add the exact redirect URL Shopify expects.

Add the exact callback URL in Shopify. Open publishing service → /auth/shopify/status and copy redirect_uri into Partners → App setup → Redirect URLs (or Dev Dashboard → your app version), save, and activate the app version.

You’ll be sent to Shopify to sign in and approve access. SyncLyst can then create and update product listings. You approve every listing before it goes live.
