How to post

How to post a listing

  1. Upload a photo (phone or this computer)
  2. Pick a marketplace in SyncLyst
  3. Open the marketplace editor tab (Add product / New listing)
  4. Come back here and tap Fill listing
  5. Review then Save/Publish on the marketplace page

SyncLyst can only fill forms on the marketplace editor tab you have open. If nothing happens, make sure you’re on the Add product / New listing page, refresh that tab, and try again. Keep the editor tab in the same Chrome window.
